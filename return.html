Return Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Service Return Policy

As a provider of software development services, this Return Policy outlines the terms under which clients may request refunds or adjustments to completed work.

Project-Based Services

For custom development projects, the following terms apply:

Initial Consultation

The initial consultation phase is non-refundable once completed. This includes project scoping, requirement gathering, and technical planning sessions.

Development Milestones

Payment for completed and approved milestones is non-refundable. Clients have the opportunity to review and request revisions during the development process before final approval.

Revision Requests

Each project includes a specified number of revision rounds as outlined in the project agreement. Additional revisions beyond the agreed scope may incur additional charges.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered in the following circumstances:

  • Failure to deliver services as specified in the project agreement
  • Significant delays caused by the service provider without valid justification
  • Major deviations from agreed specifications without client approval
  • Technical issues that cannot be resolved within a reasonable timeframe

Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable:

  • Completed and approved work
  • Third-party services or licenses purchased on behalf of the client
  • Time spent on revisions beyond the agreed scope
  • Consultation fees and initial planning sessions

Cancellation Policy

Client-Initiated Cancellation

Clients may cancel a project at any time. In such cases:

  • Payment for all completed work up to the cancellation date is due
  • Any work in progress will be billed on a prorated basis
  • Refunds for unused portions of advance payments will be processed within 14 business days

Provider-Initiated Cancellation

In rare cases where the service provider must cancel a project, clients will receive a full refund for any undelivered work.

Dispute Resolution

In case of disputes regarding services or refunds:

  1. Contact us directly to discuss the issue
  2. Provide detailed documentation of concerns
  3. Allow 5 business days for initial response and investigation
  4. If resolution cannot be reached, mediation may be pursued

Quality Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our work. If you encounter significant bugs or issues within 30 days of project completion, we will address them at no additional cost, provided they relate to the original project specifications.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed using the original payment method within 14 business days. Please note that it may take additional time for the refund to appear in your account depending on your financial institution.

Contact for Refund Requests

To initiate a refund request or discuss concerns about completed work, please contact us at:

Email: alaa1190a@gmail.com

Please include your project details, payment information, and a clear explanation of your concerns.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Return Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this website. Your continued use of our services following the posting of changes constitutes acceptance of those changes.
